You generate one flamegraph for **single-session** and one for **2-thread parallel**, then compare them side-by-side. The difference tells you exactly what changed.

## Why Your Supervisor Wants This

Without profiling, your paper says: *"Parallel is faster/slower."*

With profiling, your paper says: *"Parallel reduces per-event Rete cost by 3Г— (smaller network), but the gain is consumed by 11% queue contention + 2Г— GC pressure from event duplication. The dominant bottleneck shifts from beta-join (single-thread) to synchronization overhead (parallel)."*

That's the difference between an observation and an **explanation**. Reviewers want the explanation.
